Our Sri Lanka 8 Days Classic Tour offers an immersive journey through the island's cultural, natural, and scenic highlights. Over 7 nights and 8 days, this tour covers key attractions in Sri Lanka's cultural triangle, central highlands, and southern coastal areas. Activities include wildlife safaris, rock climbing, exploring waterfalls, boat rides, swimming, and surfing.

Day 1: Arrival and Hotel Check-in at Sigiriya

Upon your arrival at the Bandaranaike International Airport, our representative will warmly welcome you. From here, we will take you to Sigiriya, where you can check into your hotel. In the afternoon, we will visit the Dambulla Cave Temple, a UNESCO World Heritage site adorned with ancient Buddhist murals and statues.

Day 2: Sigiriya to Polonnaruwa and Back

Start the day with an early morning climb up Sigiriya Rock Fortress, an iconic landmark offering panoramic views of the surrounding area. After breakfast, we will take you to Polonnaruwa, the medieval capital of Sri Lanka, to explore its ancient ruins, including the Gal Vihara Buddha statues and the Parakrama Samudra reservoir.

Return to Sigiriya for an overnight stay.

Day 3: Sigiriya to Kandy

We will depart for Kandy after breakfast. Along the way, we will stop at the Matale Spice Garden to give you a glimpse into Sri Lanka's rich history of spice cultivation. Upon reaching Kandy, we will visit the Temple of the Tooth Relic, a sacred Buddhist site. Spend the evening enjoying a traditional cultural dance performance.

Overnight stay in Kandy.

Day 4: Kandy City Tour

Explore Kandy with us as we take you to the Peradeniya Botanical Gardens, home to exotic flora and the giant Javan fig tree. Enjoy a stroll around Kandy Lake and explore the bustling local markets. Spend your evening relaxing at the hotel or engaging in leisure activities.

Day 5: Kandy to Ella via Nuwara Eliya

We will leave for Nuwara Eliya, the "Little England" of Sri Lanka, famous for its colonial charm and tea plantations. Visit Ramboda Falls and a tea factory en route. Continue to Ella by train, a scenic journey offering breathtaking views of misty mountains and lush greenery.

Overnight stay in Ella.

Lonely Planethas ranked the Colombo-Badulla train journey as the 7th best in the world for 2025. This recognition highlights the route's unparalleled scenic beauty and the immersive cultural experience it offers for travelers.

Day 6: Ella to Yala

Start your day with a trek to Little Adam's Peak for stunning sunrise views. Visit the iconic Nine Arches Bridge before we take you to Ravana Falls. We will then proceed to Yala, where you will have the opportunity for an afternoon safari at Yala National Park, renowned for its diverse wildlife, including leopards, elephants, and colorful bird species.

Overnight stay in Yala.

Day 7: Yala to Mirissa

After breakfast, we will take you to the coastal town of Mirissa. Spend the day at leisure on the beach, enjoying activities like swimming and surfing. Visit Coconut Tree Hill, a picturesque palm grove overlooking the sea, during sunset.

Overnight stay in Mirissa.

Day 8: Departure via Galle

On your final day, we will take you to Galle to explore the historic Galle Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage site with colonial architecture and charming boutiques. Afterward, we will proceed to the airport for your departure, ensuring you leave with unforgettable memories of Sri Lanka.
