14 Days Tour

Day 1: Arrival in Negombo

Upon arrival at Bandaranaike International Airport, you will be greeted by our representative and transferred to your hotel in the seaside city of Negombo. Enjoy the rest of the day at leisure, exploring the city or relaxing by the beach.

Day 2: Negombo Sightseeing

Discover Negombo, a city famed for its fishing heritage. Visit the bustling fishing village to learn about local traditions, explore the historic Dutch Fort and Clock Tower, and relax on the sandy beaches of Negombo. Thrill-seekers can indulge in various water sports. Return to your hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 3: Negombo to Anuradhapura

Journey to Anuradhapura, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and ancient capital of Sri Lanka. Highlights include:

Mihintale: The cradle of Buddhism in Sri Lanka, surrounded by serene landscapes and ancient ruins.Atamasthana (Eight Sacred Places): Explore iconic landmarks such as Jaya Sri Maha Bodhi (a sapling of the original Bodhi tree under which Buddha attained enlightenment), Ruwanwelisaya, and Thuparamaya.

  • Mihintale: The cradle of Buddhism in Sri Lanka, surrounded by serene landscapes and ancient ruins.
  • Atamasthana (Eight Sacred Places): Explore iconic landmarks such as Jaya Sri Maha Bodhi (a sapling of the original Bodhi tree under which Buddha attained enlightenment), Ruwanwelisaya, and Thuparamaya.

End the day with a drive to your hotel in Sigiriya.

Day 4: Polonnaruwa and Minneriya National Park

Begin with a visit to Polonnaruwa, Sri Lanka's second ancient capital, renowned for its archaeological wonders, including temples, palaces, and King Parakramabahu's garden city. Later, head to Minneriya National Park to witness herds of wild elephants and other wildlife in their natural habitat.

Return to Sigiriya for an overnight stay.

Day 5: Sigiriya to Kandy

Start the day with a visit to the iconicSigiriya Rock Fortress("Lion Rock"), a UNESCO World Heritage Site. On the way to Kandy, explore theDambulla Cave Temple, adorned with murals and Buddha statues, and enjoy a tour of a spice garden.

End the day with a visit to the Matale Muthumariamman Temple before arriving in Kandy for an overnight stay.

Day 6: Kandy City Tour

Explore Kandy, the cultural capital of Sri Lanka:

Temple of the Tooth Relic (Sri Dalada Maligawa): A sacred Buddhist site.Kandy Lake: Enjoy a leisurely stroll around the serene lake.Bahirawakanda Temple: Admire the massive Buddha statue.National Gems and Gemmological Museum: Learn about Sri Lanka's rich gem history.Cultural Dance Show: Witness traditional Sri Lankan performances.

  • Temple of the Tooth Relic (Sri Dalada Maligawa): A sacred Buddhist site.
  • Kandy Lake: Enjoy a leisurely stroll around the serene lake.
  • Bahirawakanda Temple: Admire the massive Buddha statue.
  • National Gems and Gemmological Museum: Learn about Sri Lanka's rich gem history.
  • Cultural Dance Show: Witness traditional Sri Lankan performances.

Return to your hotel for the night.

'Dalada Maligawa',Temple of the Tooth Relicis located in the Kingdom ofKandy, which houses the relic of thetoothof the Lord Buddha.

Day 7: Kandy to Pinnawala and Back

Visit thePinnawala Elephant Orphanageto observe and interact with rescued elephants. On your way back, explore the intricately carvedEmbekka Devalayaand the historicGadaladeniya Temple.

Return to Kandy for your overnight stay.

Day 8: Kandy to Adam's Peak

Travel to Adam's Peak, a revered pilgrimage site. En route, stop at theDamro Labookellie Tea Centerto learn about tea production and enjoy a tasting. Embark on the climb to Adam's Peak, known for its breathtaking sunrise views.

Overnight in Adam's Peak.

Day 9: Adam's Peak to Nuwara Eliya

Head to Nuwara Eliya, with stops at scenic waterfalls, includingSt. Clair's FallsandDevon Falls. Enjoy a relaxing evening in this charming hill station.

Day 10: Nuwara Eliya to Ella by Train

Begin the day with visits toGregory Lakeand the historicNuwara Eliya Post Office. Then board the scenic train to Ella, renowned for its lush tea estates and breathtaking landscapes.

Upon arrival, transfer to your hotel in Ella.

Day 11: Ella to Yala

VisitLittle Adam's Peakand other attractions in Ella before heading to Yala. Enjoy an evening of relaxation or explore the area around your accommodation.

Day 12: Yala Safari and Transfer to Mirissa

Experience a thrilling wildlife safari inYala National Park, home to leopards, elephants, and diverse fauna. Afterward, travel to the coastal town of Mirissa for an overnight stay.

Day 13: Mirissa to Bentota

In the morning, enjoy whale watching in Mirissa (seasonal). Continue to Bentota, where you can relax on pristine beaches or indulge in water sports.

Stay overnight in Bentota.

Day 14: Bentota to Colombo and Departure

Visit Colombo for a city tour, exploring landmarks such as theGalle Face Green,Independence Square, andGangaramaya Temple. After shopping or enjoying local cuisine, transfer to the airport for your departure.
