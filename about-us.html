About Us

Sri Lanka's rich culture, stunning landscapes, and warm hospitality.

With over 30 years in the hospitality industry and more than a decade of experience as tour agents, we are proud to be a trusted partner in helping you explore the beauty of Sri Lanka.

Our Journey

Our story began with the Pinnalanda Group, famous for its connection to the renowned Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage. What started as a small inn offering nature lovers a close-up view of the orphanage soon blossomed into a thriving family-run business. Today, the Pinnalanda Group boasts a stunning restaurant, hotel, and bungalow setup across three picturesque locations in Sri Lanka's Sabaragamuwa and Central Provinces.

Our humble beginnings are rooted in a passion for nature and culture, and this ethos continues to inspire us as we serve travelers from all corners of the world.
